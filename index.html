Who is Sunny?

I’m SUNNY, a graphic designer who treats pixels like stardust and deadlines like plot twists. My work lives somewhere between the cosmic and the everyday, where a color palette can feel like a good cup of coffee and a font choice can change the mood of an entire galaxy. I collect odd inspirations (like street signs that accidentally rhyme) and believe good design should be as memorable as a song stuck in your head. Think of me as your friendly design astronaut, ready to launch ideas into orbit, but always with both feet planted firmly in creativity.
