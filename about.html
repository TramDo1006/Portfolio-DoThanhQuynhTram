ABOUT ME

I’m Sunny, a graphic designer whose work blends clean, intentional design with a cosmic sense of wonder. Like mapping constellations, I connect ideas into visual stories, from branding and identities to playful illustrations and bold layouts. Inspired by light, color, and the rhythms of the cosmos, I create designs that feel timeless yet alive, lingering in memory like a glimpse of the Milky Way.

"Design in lightyears, deliver in seconds."
